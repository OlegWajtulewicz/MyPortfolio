About me

Greetings! I am a musician and percussionist who has always strived for perfection and new challenges. Music is my calling, my passion, and a source of inspiration.
Some time ago, I started exploring front-end development and found a new passion that allowed me to unlock new creative abilities. Front-end development enables me to bring my ideas to life, create beautiful and functional websites and applications that are not only aesthetically appealing, but also easy to use.

While I continue to play percussion, I am constantly amazed at how music and development interact, allowing me to find new ways to express my thoughts and ideas.
I am committed and ready for new challenges in both of my passions - music and web development.
